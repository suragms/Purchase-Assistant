# ROLE BASED WORKFLOW

## Owner
- Monitor low/out/disputed/verification alerts.
- Approve corrections and reorder actions.
- Review delivery and mismatch escalations.

## Manager
- Receive approval/task escalation notifications.
- Validate verification/delivery exceptions.

## Staff
- Mark delivery and perform warehouse verification.
- Submit physical counts and corrective actions.
- Complete daily checklist tasks with immediate feedback.

## Current Gaps
- Partial delivery verification does not yet drive stock delta in backend lifecycle.
